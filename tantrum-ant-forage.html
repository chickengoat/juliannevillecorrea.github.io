By Julian Correa - Published on November 15, 2024

i forage now i foraged then
many foliage, carcus & grain
from time old to time new
from sweet honey to fresh dew

i do not know any ant
that would say i cant
we are a different breed
work work work our creed

ancestors solid as rock
well this no loose talk
only way the story told
we are the protagonists bold
